The Queen is Dead Album

"The Queen Is Dead" by The Smiths, released in 1986, is a timeless masterpiece that showcases Morrissey's poignant lyrics and Johnny Marr's jangly guitar work. With iconic tracks like the title song and "There Is a Light That Never Goes Out," the album remains a pinnacle of 1980s alternative rock.

Meat is Murder

"Meat Is Murder" (1985) by The Smiths is a seminal album known for its bold political themes, particularly addressing animal rights. With tracks like the title song, the album showcases Morrissey's passionate lyrics and Johnny Marr's distinctive guitar work, cementing its status as a socially conscious classic.
